About the Programming Club

Programming is a skill that is valued highly among multiple career choices including but not limited to most of the engineering fields. However, even for those who are not planning to study engineering, computer science is still a valuable field to learn about. Programming is a tool that can be used to automize many rigorous tasks and give developers unrestrictive access to what they can create. When placed in the right hands, programming can lead to the creation of both artistic and usefull works. Most importantly, programming teaches valuable life skills such as problem solving, creativity, and critical thinking. At the Programming Club, we will mostly be focused on these 3 essential aspects of programming. We will be looking into methods to tackle difficult problems and learn algorithms that aid towards making fast and efficient programs. The Programming Club will also be a great expirience to learn how to work as a team and how to create production ready code. We will be working on group projects, such as developing the ASFM CCE Website, use widely popular, colaborative tools, such as git, and work to re-establish the yearly hackathon competition.

Should I Join?

If you have ever felt curious to learn about programming, or just want to explore the opportunity to learn about something new, than you would make a great fit in the Programming Club.
